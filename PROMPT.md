## Business Description

**Ria's Cuisine** is a professional catering company specializing in authentic African cuisine while also offering customized catering services for all types of events.

The company serves:

* Weddings
* Birthdays
* Corporate Events
* Conferences
* Family Gatherings
* Festivals
* Private Parties
* Community Events
* School Events
* Religious Celebrations
* Anniversaries

Ria's Cuisine provides high-quality food, professional service, and customized menus tailored to each event.
